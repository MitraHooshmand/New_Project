Persian Carpet

hand made carpet nice and colored

Persian carpet is a heavy textile made for a wide variety of utilitarian and symbolic purposes and produced in Iran. Carpet weaving is an essential part of Persian culture and Iranian art. Within the group of Oriental rugs produced by the countries of the "rug belt", the Persian carpet stands out by the variety and elaborateness of its manifold designs.
